What is CaféKit?

An overview of the platform and who it's for

CaféKit is an all-in-one business platform designed to help new café business owners get started. It guides users through a complete onboarding process — from submitting business documents and attending training to purchasing the necessary products and supplies.

Think of CaféKit as your dedicated support system. The MYO team uses the platform to review submissions, schedule training, and assist clients at every stage.

Who Uses CaféKit?

Business Owner (Client)

A new café owner going through the onboarding program.

Business Owner (Customer)

A café owner who has completed their service and purchases products for their business.

Administrator

MYO staff who manage clients, review submissions, schedule training, and handle orders.

Note: When you register, you are automatically set up as a Business Owner. You do not need to select a role manually.

Getting Started

Create your account and log in for the first time

Creating Your Account

If you're new to CaféKit, you'll need to register first.

  1. Visit the CaféKit website and click Register.
  2. Fill in your personal and business details:
    • First and last name
    • Email address (this will be your login)
    • Password
    • Business name, phone number, and address
  3. Click Submit.

Logging In

  1. Go to the CaféKit website and click Login.
  2. Enter your email address and password.
  3. Click Login to access your dashboard.

For Business Owners (Clients)

Everything you need to know as a new café business owner

3.1 Your Dashboard

When you log in, you'll land on your Home Dashboard. This is your main hub, showing:

  • Onboarding progress
  • Quick access to features
Note: If you have not yet availed any services offered by CaféKit, you will be instructed to avail a service first before starting your onboarding process.

Browsing Services

Go to Place New Order to view what CaféKit offers. You can see what's included, view pricing, purchase a service, and track services you've already availed.

3.2 The Onboarding Journey (9 Steps)

The onboarding process is your guided path to launching your café. You work through the steps in order, and the MYO team supports you along the way.

Step 1
Client Sign Up

Submit your basic business and owner information. Also submit your required compliance documents and business requirements. The MYO team will review these and either approve them or reject (request changes). If changes are needed, you'll see a note explaining what to fix. Simply correct and resubmit.

Step 2
Brand Making / Layout

Communicate with the MYO team regarding the branding materials using Messages. Upload photos and documents showing your café's setup progress (water, electricity, interior, etc.) so that the team knows that you are prepared for future training.

Step 3
Onboarding Process

You will attend the official onboarding meeting where the MYO team will discuss your package inclusions, project timeline, design process, training expectations, and important preparation guidelines. After the session, you will be completing the acknowledgement form.

Step 4
Package and Inclusions

View the service package assigned to your business and track which inclusions have been completed.

Step 5
Café Progress & Final Billing

Upload photos and documents showing your café's setup progress (water, electricity, interior, etc.) and settle your final billing if you still have an outstanding balance.

Step 6
Finalization of Training Dates

View the training schedule agreed upon by you and the MYO team.

Step 7
Things to Prepare

Review a checklist of everything you need to prepare before your training week begins. The checklist needs to be fulfilled before undergoing training.

Step 8
Training Week

Attend your scheduled training sessions — either Face-to-Face or Online.

Step 9
Service Evaluation

Complete a short evaluation form to rate your onboarding and training experience. Your feedback helps the MYO team improve its service.

What happens after Step 9? Once all steps are complete and your service payment is confirmed, your account will be fully activated and you'll gain access to the CaféKit marketplace.

3.3 Shopping for Products & Services

Important: You can only purchase CaféKit's offered products after your onboarding is complete and your service payment has been confirmed. After this, you can no longer avail another service and only the products will be available for purchase.

Browsing Products

  1. From your dashboard, go to Place New Order.
  2. Browse by category or use the search bar to find items.
  3. Click any product to see images, description, variants, pricing, and stock status.

Adding Items to Your Cart

  1. Select the variant you want (e.g., size or type).
  2. Select quantity.
  3. Click Add to Cart.
  4. Click the cart icon at the top of the page to view your cart.

Checking Out

  1. Review the items in your cart and click Checkout.
  2. Enter your delivery address.
  3. Review your order summary.
  4. Choose a payment method: Maya or PayPal.
  5. Tick the order Terms and Agreement.
  6. Complete payment — you'll receive a confirmation when it goes through.

3.5 Cancellations & Refunds

Important note: Cancelling an order is only valid for 24 hours after ordering and paying. Beyond that timeframe, you cannot cancel anymore.

Cancelling an Order

You can request a cancellation before your order has been shipped.

  1. Go to My Orders and open the order.
  2. Click Cancel Order.
  3. Select a reason (e.g., changed my mind, found cheaper alternatives, quality concerns, etc.).
  4. Click Submit. Wait for the refund of your cancelled order.

Requesting a Refund

If there's an issue with a delivered order:

  1. Go to My Orders and open the order.
  2. Click Request Return/Refund.
  3. Select the items you want refunded and provide a reason.
  4. Attach supporting photos or videos.
  5. Click Submit.

For Administrators

Managing clients, products, orders, and platform content

4.1 Admin Dashboard Overview

When you log in as an administrator, you'll see the Admin Dashboard — your control center for managing all CaféKit clients, products, services, and operations.

From here you can quickly navigate to:

  • Client management and business profiles
  • Analytics on Business Performance
  • The Project Workspace (onboarding oversight)
  • Product and service catalog
  • Order management and refunds
  • Reports, settings, and content

4.2 Managing Clients

Go to Business Profiles to see a list of all registered client businesses.

Viewing a Client Profile

Click on any business to see owner details, business information, assigned service, and payment/completion status.

Editing a Client Profile

From the business profile page, you can:

  • Edit owner information (name, email, contact)
  • Edit business information (name, address, description)
  • Upload or change the business logo

4.3 Project Workspace

The Project Workspace is where you manage the entire client onboarding process. It is organized into six tabs:

Tab 1 — Client Signup

Review client onboarding submissions. You can search and sort, then Approve or Reject each submission. When rejecting, add a note so the client knows what to fix.

Tab 2 — Store Progress

Monitor café setup progress updates (water, electricity, interior) submitted by clients. View their uploaded documents and photos.

Tab 3 — Acknowledgement Forms

View and download acknowledgement form submissions from clients. Track which businesses have submitted their forms.

Tab 4 — Package and Inclusions

View all service package inclusions assigned to each business. Update each inclusion's status to Pending or Completed as work progresses.

Tab 5 — Things to Prepare

Manage the pre-training checklist for each business and monitor what clients still need to complete before training begins.

Tab 6 — Training

Schedule and manage training sessions:

  1. Click Add Training.
  2. Select the business, set the training date and session number, and choose the mode (Face-to-Face or Online).
  3. Save the session. Track status as Pending or Completed.

4.4 Managing Products

Product Categories

Go to Products > Categories to create, activate/deactivate, or delete categories.

Products

Go to Products > All Products to:

  • Create a product with a name, description, category, and images
  • Search and sort your product list
  • Delete products (they are hidden from clients, not permanently removed)

Product Variants

Each product can have multiple variants (e.g., different sizes). Open a product and go to its Variants section to add variants with a name, price, weight, and stock quantity. You can also activate, deactivate, or delete individual variants.

4.5 Managing Services

Creating a Service

  1. Go to Services and click Add Service.
  2. Fill in the title, description, price, and upload an image.
  3. Click Save.

Service Inclusions

Each service has a list of included items. Open a service, go to Inclusions, and add, edit, or delete inclusion items. These are what clients see and track in Step 4 of their onboarding.

4.6 Managing Orders

Go to View Orders to view and manage all orders placed by all clients.

Viewing and Updating Orders

  • Browse all orders, search and filter as needed
  • Click an order to see full details (items, pricing, delivery, payment)
  • Update the order status as it progresses (e.g., mark as Ready for Shipment)

Managing Refund Requests

Go to Return/Refund Requests to view the orders that clients want to return/refund.

  1. Open an order that has a refund request.
  2. Review the reason and the attached evidence.
  3. Click Approve or Reject.

4.7 Managing Employees

Go to My Employees to manage your team.

  • Add a new employee by entering their name, email, and role
  • Edit employee details as needed
  • Delete an employee account when no longer needed

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Q I just registered. What should I do first?
After registering, you'll be taken to your onboarding dashboard. Start with Step 1 (Client Sign Up) and work your way through each step in order. The platform will guide you.
Q I submitted a document but it was rejected. What do I do?
Check the admin's note on the rejected submission — it will explain what needs to be corrected. Make the necessary changes and resubmit. The MYO team will review it again.
Q Why can't I see products in the marketplace?
The marketplace is only unlocked after you have completed all onboarding steps and your service payment has been confirmed. If you believe your account should already be active, contact the MYO team.
Q How do I know when my order is coming?
Go to My Orders and click on your order to see its current status. The status tracker will show you exactly where your order is in the process — from preparation all the way to delivery.
Q I want to cancel my order. Is it too late?
Cancellations are only valid within 24 hours of ordering and paying. If that window has passed, you will need to request a refund after delivery instead.
Q How do I contact the MYO team?
You can reach the team through the Messages section in your dashboard, or by using the Contact Us page on the CaféKit website.
Q (Admin) How do I schedule a training session for a client?
Go to the Project Workspace, click the Training tab, then click Add Training. Select the business, set the date, session number, and training mode (Face-to-Face or Online), then save.
Q (Admin) I deleted a product but it still shows in my records. Why?
Products are soft-deleted — they are hidden from clients but kept in the system for record-keeping purposes. This protects the integrity of past orders and data.
